Activity 2.5.2.

Let \(f(x)=\sqrt{x+1}\) and \(g(x)= 5x\text{.}\)
(a)
Which of the following seems likely to be the most simplified form of \(f(x)\cdot g(x)\text{?}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{5x+1} \)
  2. \(\displaystyle 5\sqrt{x+1}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{5x^2+5x}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle 5x\sqrt{x+1}\)
(b)
Which of the following seems likely to be the most simplified form of \(\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)}\text{?}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{5x}{\sqrt{x+1}}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{\sqrt{x+1}}{5x}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{\dfrac{x}{5x}+\dfrac{1}{5x}} \)
  4. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{\dfrac{5x}{x}+\dfrac{5x}{1}} \)
