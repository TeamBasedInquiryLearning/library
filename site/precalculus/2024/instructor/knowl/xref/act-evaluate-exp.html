Activity 5.1.5.

Evaluate the following exponential functions.
(a)
\(f(x)=4^{x}\) for \(f(3)\)
Answer.
\(f(3)=64\)
(b)
\(f(x)=\left( \dfrac{1}{3} \right)^{x}\) for \(f(3)\)
Answer.
\(f(3)=\dfrac{1}{27}\)
(c)
\(f(x)=3\left( 5 \right)^{x}\) for \(f(-2)\)
Answer.
\(f(-2)=\dfrac{3}{25}\)
(d)
\(f(x)=-2^{3x-4}\) for \(f(4)\)
Answer.
\(f(4)=-256\)
