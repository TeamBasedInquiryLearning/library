Activity 2.5.1.

Let \(f(x)=x^2-3x\) and \(g(x)= x^3-4x^2+7\text{.}\)
(a)
Which of the following seems likely to be the most simplified form of \(f(x)+g(x)\text{?}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle x^2-3x+x^3-4x^2+7 \)
  2. \(\displaystyle x^5-7x^3+7\)
  3. \(\displaystyle -x^3+5x^2-3x-7 \)
  4. \(\displaystyle x^3-3x^2-3x+7\)
Answer.
D
(b)
Which of the following seems likely to be the most simplified form of \(f(x)-g(x)\text{?}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle x^3-3x^2-3x+7 \)
  2. \(\displaystyle -x^3+5x^2-3x-7\)
  3. \(\displaystyle -x^3-3x^2-3x+7 \)
  4. \(\displaystyle x^2-3x-x^3+4x^2-7\)
Answer.
B
